# Guarantee Snippet: Pipeline ROI

**Usage:** Use when proposal includes performance marketing or lead generation. Replace [X] and [Y] with agreed metrics from the call.

**Text:**
> We're not in the business of billing you for activity. If the system we build doesn't generate at least [X] qualified leads within the first [Y] days of going live, we continue working — at our cost — until it does. We only win when you do.
