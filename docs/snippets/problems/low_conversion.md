# Problem Snippet: Low Conversion

**icon_key:** `low_conversion`

**Title options:**
- "Traffic Exists, Conversions Don't"
- "Strong Reach, Weak Revenue Capture"
- "Awareness Isn't Turning Into Sales"

**Desc template:**
> The brand has visibility — content, followers, ad spend — but the funnel breaks somewhere between interest and purchase. Without clear CTAs, retargeting sequences, or a nurture strategy, you're paying to attract attention you're not keeping.

**Usage:** Best when prospect mentioned ads running but sales not matching, social following not converting, or website traffic without revenue.
